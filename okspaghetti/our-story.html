Our Story

My Danish grandmother had a passion for cooking Italian food even though she never visited Italy even once in her long life. She passed this passion onto me. I spent much of my childhood over at Grandma’s eating her food, and eventually learning these same recipes that you can now enjoy.

After going to prison for digital piracy, I decided it was time to clean up my act and decided to try creating the next big phone game à la Flappy Bird. After this failed, I decided to go into a more reliable industry and started the restaurant that my grandmother had always dreamed of and OK! Spaghetti was born.

We hope that our food will call back your own memories of family dinners just as they do for us.
