Our Menu

Welcome to the menu, here you will find all of our delicious meals, from our signature spaghetti all the way to kids’ meal items like chicken fingers. We hope that you find everything you are looking for!

Entrées

The OK! Spaghetti

The signature dish of our fine restaurant the recipe was passed down to from generation to generation (my grandmother’s generation to mine), if you don’t know what spaghetti is, google it you obviously have access to the internet if you have found this website.

Chicken Scarpariello

Chicken sauteed in a tangly lemon glaze with bell pepper. This dish is cooked on the stove for hours to bring out the delicious Italian flavor. Due to the long cooking time we might be out depending on when you order.

Agnolotti del Plin

Tiny, meat filled Piedmontese agnolotti, stuffed with a pork, chicken, veal, and vegetable filling. Bound with butter and cheese.

Pork Chop Milanese

A rib-cut pork chop dredged in flour, eggs, and seasoned with breadcrumbs before being fried. (the pork can be substituted with beef, or chicken).

Ratatouille

Yes, it is the meal that the rat made. As my grandmother was wont to say, “French people are just rude Italians.”

Fettuccine Alfredo

A pasta dish made with fettuccine, butter, and parmesan cheese sauce.

Soups

Ribollita

A Tuscan bread soup, made with cannellini beans, laccarrot, beans, chard, celery, potatoes and onion.

Minestrone

A thick soup made with white beans, onions, celery, carrots, leaf vegetables, and tomatoes, and pasta.

Pappa al Pomodoro

This hearty tomato-bread soup starts with a whopping five pounds of fresh tomatoes. The tomatoes simmer with onion, crushed red pepper, and garlic. They then get thickened with cubes of Italian bread, and served with a drizzle of olive oil and a dollop of ricotta.
